FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the questions we get asked most often

What is included in the trekking package?

The trekking package includes:

  • ✓ Permit (National Park Entrance Permit)
  • ✓ Guide Association Fee (Professional Guide)
  • ✓ Food (Meals during the trek)
  • ✓ Drinks
  • ✓ Fresh Fruits

Not included: Video Camera Tax (€10) and Tube Rafting (€10)

Is it safe for beginners?

Absolutely! Our guides are experienced professionals specially trained to assist first-time trekkers. We also provide complete safety equipment, including first aid kits, communication radios, and travel insurance.

Are there any additional fees?

Additional fees that may apply:

  • ✓ Permit PIPPH Office (Video Camera Tax) → €10
  • ✓ Tube Rafting → €10
  • ✓ Tips for guides (optional)

All additional charges will be clearly explained before departure.

What should I bring?

Recommended items to bring:

  • ✓ Comfortable trekking shoes
  • ✓ Extra clothes & a light jacket
  • ✓ Sunscreen & a hat
  • ✓ A camera to capture unforgettable moments
  • ✓ Personal medication if needed

How long does the trekking take?

We offer various trekking durations:

  • ✓ 3 Hours Trek (Short Trek)
  • ✓ One Day Trek
  • ✓ Two Day Trek (Including Camping)
  • ✓ Three to Seven Day Trek

You can choose the option that best suits your fitness level and schedule.
